The Code Book gives filed information about the data in tidy.txt.

Field 1: suject - The ID of the test object.
Field 2: activity - The type of activity for each measurement, including:
    1 - WALKING
    2 - WALKING_UPSTAIRS
    3 - WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
    4 - SITTING
    5 - STANDING
    6 - LAYING
